Yo Viajo is the smart way to travel by bus in Costa Rica. This application aims to help the travelers inside the country, as well as to share their experience with others. Matilandia

Parents of a group of school students decided to give their children a more interactive way to learn, Matilandia is a questions game created for children, the goal is to study after class anywhere, anytime, it is easy to use, and it helps to identify areas in which the kid needs a little push.

Golveca

Golveca is a company specialized in importations and distributions, located in Costa Rica. Their catalog of more than 1000 products used to be a folder with printed products images, their sales agents were having troubles to handle the catalog in a efficient way. The solution was to create a tablet optimized application. The sales agents can now show the products in a easier manner, helping the environment by reducing the amount of paper and increasing sales using custom analytics.

Yo Denuncio

Currently, in Costa Rica arises the problem of excessive procedures and protocols in the Government institutions to handle complaints or warnings of potential situations of risk for the most vulnerable populations in the country. Yo Denuncio is free tool offered to the government where the citizens can make complaints from a single place.
